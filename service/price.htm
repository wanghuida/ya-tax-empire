转让定价（TRANSFER PRICING SERVICES）

转让定价风险与机会评估；审核和评估客户转让定价现状，提供减缓风险和筹划机会的实务方案。
转让定价基础分析与标准研究：对客户与关联公司之间交易方式、价格构成进行分析，与第三方价格对比分析，提供恰当的转让定价制订基础与标准，以备税务机关调查。
预约定价：协助客户标准预约定价方案，协助与税务机关商谈，以期由税务机关确认适当的转让预约价格，避免潜在转让定价风险。

We shall provide our clients with transfer pricing risk and opportunity assessment. We shall audit and evaluate our clients’ present condition of transfer pricing and provide them with practical plans to mitigate the risks and explore opportunity.
We shall provide our clients with fundamental analysis and bench marking study.We shall analyze the trade models and price formation between our clients and their related companies and contrast with the prices with the third part before we provide our clients with proper fundaments and standards for making transfer pricing and for the inspection by the tax authorities.
We shall provide our clients with advance pricing agreement(“APA”)services.We shall help our clients to prepare for the APA plan and assist them to negotiate with tax authorities so as to attain the confirmation of the advance pricing by tax authorities and avoid the potential risks of transfer pricing.
