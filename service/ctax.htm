代理企业所得税汇算清缴 (ACTING FOR ENTERPRISE INCOME TAX FINAL SETTLEMENT)

根据纳税人提供的财务会计报表和纳税申报资料，依照税法规定审核各税种的计算与缴纳；审核确认企业所得税税前扣除项目；审核确认纳税调整与应纳税所得额，出具当地税务机关认可的具有法律效力的代理企业所得税汇算清缴报告。

We shall examine the tax calculation and payment of tax in line with the finance statement and tax returns provided by the taxpayers and examine the deduction items of the income tax,and examine tax adjustment and taxable income before we issue legal tax report recognized by relevant tax authorities.
