业务介绍

税务培训

客户办税人员提供税务培训服务；举办税收专题研讨会；配合国、地税机关宣传税收法规需要举办各类大型税务培训活动。

税务申报代理 (ACTING FOR FILING TAX RETURNS)

根据各税种的法定申报要求，依据企业不同类型、性质、不同经营内容提供增值税、营业税、企业所得税等各税种申报代理服务。 

We shall provide our clients with filing tax returns services based on tax laws requirements.According to the nature and operation of different enterprises,we shall file the returns of the value added tax or businesss tax or income tax or other taxes for clients.

 

个人所得税代理 (ACTING FOR INDIVIDUAL INCOME TAX)

提供个人所得税涉税事宜的咨询与筹划；个人所得税申报代理服务。 

We shall provide our clients with concultancy and planning involving with individual income tax and act for the filing of individual income tax returns.

 

代理企业所得税汇算清缴 (ACTING FOR ENTERPRISE INCOME TAX FINAL SETTLEMENT)

根据纳税人提供的财务会计报表和纳税申报资料，依照税法规定审核各税种的计算与缴纳；审核确认企业所得税税前扣除项目；审核确认纳税调整与应纳税所得额，出具当地税务机关认可的具有法律效力的代理企业所得税汇算清缴报告。 

We shall examine the tax calculation and payment of tax in line with the finance statement and tax returns provided by the taxpayers and examine the deduction items of the income tax,and examine tax adjustment and taxable income before we issue legal tax report recognized by relevant tax authorities.

 

税政协助

协助纳税人处理涉税争议；当纳税人遭遇税制缺陷，代理纳税人向相应权限国家部委陈情沟通，推动纳税人利益的法律保护。

 

转让定价（TRANSFER PRICING SERVICES）

转让定价风险与机会评估；审核和评估客户转让定价现状，提供减缓风险和筹划机会的实务方案。 
转让定价基础分析与标准研究：对客户与关联公司之间交易方式、价格构成进行分析，与第三方价格对比分析，提供恰当的转让定价制订基础与标准，以备税务机关调查。
预约定价：协助客户标准预约定价方案，协助与税务机关商谈，以期由税务机关确认适当的转让预约价格，避免潜在转让定价风险。 

We shall provide our clients with transfer pricing risk and opportunity assessment. We shall audit and evaluate our clients’ present condition of transfer pricing and provide them with practical plans to mitigate the risks and explore opportunity.
We shall provide our clients with fundamental analysis and bench marking study.We shall analyze the trade models and price formation between our clients and their related companies and contrast with the prices with the third part before we provide our clients with proper fundaments and standards for making transfer pricing and for the inspection by the tax authorities.
We shall provide our clients with advance pricing agreement(“APA”)services.We shall help our clients to prepare for the APA plan and assist them to negotiate with tax authorities so as to attain the confirmation of the advance pricing by tax authorities and avoid the potential risks of transfer pricing.

 

税务筹划（TAX　PLANNING）

设计优化节税方案；在不违背税法、税收规定的前提下、对存在两种以上的纳税方案进行筹划和优化选择，实现税收负担最小化，达到税后利润最大化。 
投资项目税务分析与评估；对跨国公司来华投资提供税务筹划方案、税收环境调查报告；对投资项目提供税务调查与评估服务。 
公司重组、股权转让以及资产置换过程中的税务筹划；特殊税收政策问题的呈请审批服务。 

We shall design the optimal tax plan.Complying with the tax law and regulations,we shall choose the best from two or more tax plan so as to enable our clients to minimize the tax burden and　maximize the profit after tax.
We shall make tax analysis and assessment on the invested projects.We shall design tax plan for multinational companies’ investment in china and issue investingating report on tax environment and provide with tax investingation and assessment services for the invested projects.
We shall provide companies with tax planning services in the course of their reorganization,share transfer and assets displacement.
We shall assist our clients to apply for the approval by tax authorities of incentive tax rate and some special tax polices.

 

税务审计（TAX REVIEW）

为客户提供纳税年度常规税务审计；并购、投资活动等事项税务审计与税务调查服务；揭示潜在纳税风险，对节税空间进行评估并提出合理性建议。 

We shall provide clients with regular tax review on annual basis,with specific tax review for M&A and other investment activities,and with tax investigation service in order to evaluate the potential tax risks and to explore the possibilities of saving tax and make reasonable proposals.

税务顾问（TAX CONSULTANCY）

担当各类公司、企业常年税务顾问；为客户提供涉税事项日常咨询；提供最新税收法规要点分析；定期复核客户纳税状况；协助客户与税务机关协调各项涉税事宜。 

We shall act as tax advisers for various companies,provide clients with the daily professional consultancy service on various tax affairs,analyze the latest tax laws and regulations,review and assess client’s tax liability on a regular basis and help clients negotiate with tax officials on various tax issues.
